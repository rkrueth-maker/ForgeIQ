Reusable case-study fields

Customer problem

What was not working, what outcome was needed, and why the problem mattered.

Starting condition

Verified current state, constraints, known facts, and relevant unknowns.

Information received

Photos, files, measurements, records, process data, approvals, and other authorized inputs.

Work performed

Exact planning, implementation, review, testing, documentation, or handoff work completed.

Deliverables

The actual plan, workflow, report, website, system, checklist, document, or decision package supplied.

Customer-controlled decisions

Scope, price, assumptions, approvals, purchases, implementation choices, and final acceptance controlled by the customer.

Final result

Only supported outcomes, without invented savings, rankings, performance, or guarantees.

Proof and authorization

Classification, evidence location, private-data review, customer authorization status, and approved publication date.

Publication rule: A real case study remains unpublished until the evidence, privacy review, customer authorization, wording, and proof classification are approved.
