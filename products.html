FAQ

Common questions before you send the mess.

Can I send old texts, screenshots, photos, or half-finished files?

Yes. That is the point. ForgeIQ starts with the rough details you already have and turns them into a cleaner working output.

Will ForgeIQ pick software for me?

Only if the workflow proves you need it. The first goal is to organize the details into a useful document, sheet, form, dashboard, packet, or scope.

How does Custom Work start?

You send the rough problem first. Before build work starts, you get a scope note, delivery format, quote, and boundary check.

What is not included?

ForgeIQ does not provide legal, tax, licensed engineering, permit, or trade advice. It provides planning, organizing, workflow, setup, copy, and practical output work.

Not sure where to start?

Send the mess. ForgeIQ can point it to the right ladder step, standard product, or scoped custom work build.
