# Project Planning Checklist

Use this before buying materials or starting a project.

## Send or collect
- Photos of the area
- Rough dimensions
- Desired outcome
- Budget range
- Must-have items
- Nice-to-have items
- Product links
- Deadline or seasonal need

## Check before starting
- What is the first useful action?
- What measurements are missing?
- What can wait until later?
- What needs a qualified contractor?
- What could create rework if guessed?
