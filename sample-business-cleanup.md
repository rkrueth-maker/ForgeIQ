# Business Cleanup Sample

Purpose: show how scattered customer notes become a simple operating path.

Customer sends:
- service list
- quote examples
- message screenshots
- current follow-up process

Output:
- service menu cleanup
- lead tracker fields
- quote status path
- follow-up message templates
- owner next-action list

Example statuses: new, need info, quoted, approved, deposit paid, in progress, delivered, follow-up.
