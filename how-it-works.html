Customer journey

Five clear steps.

1

Choose the outcome

Start with what needs to be clearer, planned, improved, built, quoted, or controlled. Highway 38 recommends the smallest useful service.

2

Approve the buying terms

Review price, payment, turnaround, included work, exclusions, assumptions, and revision allowance before work begins.

3

Send the information

Provide photos, measurements, notes, files, screenshots, process details, and the result you need.

4

Receive the result

Receive the approved plan, workflow, quote, decision packet, configured system, or other defined deliverable.

5

Use the included revision

Request the bounded clarification or revision included with the service, then approve any separately scoped next phase.

Control boundary

Nothing important becomes automatic.

Submitting a request creates no charge. Customer sends, financial posting, payments, payroll, tax actions, proof sharing, and publishing stay approval-controlled.
