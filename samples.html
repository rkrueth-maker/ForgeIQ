1. Project Cleanup Snapshot Sample

Example customer: Pine Ridge Property Help has messy notes about a garage cleanup, camera placement, and a small landing page idea.

Customer Sends

  • 6 garage photos
  • Rough 24' x 28' measurement
  • List of tools and storage issues
  • Text-message notes from the customer
  • Goal: make the garage usable and explain services online

Northwoods Delivers

  • Plain-English problem summary
  • Missing measurements and photo list
  • Risk check: electrical, trip hazards, weather exposure
  • Simple phase plan: clear, measure, layout, quote, build
  • Next-step checklist the owner can act on
Sales angle: This shows the $79/$99 offer clearly. Customer sees what they get before buying.

12. Garage / Shop Layout Review Sample

Photos and dimensions become a layout concept and action plan.

Inputs

  • 28' x 40' garage rough dimensions
  • Two overhead doors on short wall
  • Workbench, toolboxes, vehicles, storage goals
  • Photos from each corner

Output

  • Suggested zones: vehicle, workbench, storage, walk path
  • What needs measuring before buying shelves
  • Safety and clearance notes
  • Phase checklist: clean, measure, mark, move, review

13. Sprinkler / Irrigation Planning Sample

Yard photos, satellite screenshots, rough dimensions, and goals can become a practical planning packet.

Inputs

Satellite screenshot, yard photos, water source location, driveway, trees, fence, garden beds, watering goals.

Concept Output

Suggested watering areas, possible zone notes, head-placement ideas, trench path notes, and questions to verify.

Required Checks

Water pressure, flow rate, backflow rules, utility marking, local rules, and licensed help where required.
