## Website Launch Check

Before sharing the site publicly:

1. Confirm GitHub Pages is enabled.
   - GitHub repo → Settings → Pages
   - Source: Deploy from a branch
   - Branch: `main`
   - Folder: `/root`
   - Save
2. Open the live site.
3. Click each top button.
4. Confirm **Start a Request** opens the Google Form.
5. Submit one test form response.
6. Confirm the test response lands in the response tracker sheet.
7. Delete or mark the test response as `Test`.
8. Confirm the site does not show an unverified public email address.
9. Confirm the footer still says this is a working brand until name/domain verification is done.

## First Lead Workflow

When a new form response comes in:

1. Open **RangeRivet Works Intake Responses**.
2. Set `Status` to `New Lead`.
3. Read the service needed, outcome wanted, budget, and details.
4. Set `Priority`:
   - High = clear need, clear budget, easy service fit
   - Medium = needs more info but likely a fit
   - Low = unclear, low budget, or not a strong fit
5. Fill `Next Action`.
6. Reply using the preferred contact method.
7. Move status to one of:
   - Needs Review
   - Waiting on Info
   - Quoted
   - Approved
   - In Progress
   - Completed
   - Declined / Not Fit

## First Reply Template

Use this for a new lead:

Hi, thanks for reaching out to RangeRivet Works.

I looked over your request. This looks like it fits under **[SERVICE TYPE]**.

To give you the best next step, I need:

- Photos or links if you have them
- Measurements or rough notes if this involves a space or layout
- Device/app/product list if this involves smart-home, camera, web, or product setup
- What result you want first: plan, layout, tracker, form, AI workflow, product recommendation, or process review

Once I have that, I can recommend the right starter option and price range.

Thanks,
RangeRivet Works

## Do Not Sell Yet

Avoid these until the business proves demand:

- Full custom app development
- SaaS platform
- App store mobile app
- Complex automation buildouts
- Electrical wiring
- Panel work
- Hardwired fixture installation
- Alarm/security system contracting
- Lock installation liability
- General cell phone help as a standalone service
