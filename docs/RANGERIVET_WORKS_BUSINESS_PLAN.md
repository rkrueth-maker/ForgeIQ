# RangeRivet Works Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**RangeRivet Works** is a practical shop, garage, and small-business systems brand built around five connected offers:

1. Shop products
2. Plans and layout help
3. Job tracking setup
4. AI workflow setup
5. Shop process improvement

The business helps homeowners, garage owners, side businesses, small contractors, small shops, and fabrication/repair businesses turn messy spaces, rough ideas, scattered job information, and inefficient workflows into practical plans, cleaner systems, and better buying decisions.

**Working tagline:** Practical tools, plans, and systems for garages, shops, and small businesses.

RangeRivet Works should be treated as the working umbrella brand until the name is verified through Minnesota SOS, USPTO, domain availability, Google search, and social handle checks. ForgeIQ remains the existing Shopify/store/code asset for now and should not be permanently rebranded until name clearance is complete.

---

## Brand Position

RangeRivet Works should sound practical, mechanical, grounded, and easy to understand.

The brand should not lead with software, AI, or consulting language. It should lead with hands-on problem solving:

> Send photos, notes, measurements, quotes, or job details. RangeRivet Works helps turn them into a practical plan, cleaner quote, better layout, or simple tracking system.

## Proof-of-Concept Plan

### Phase 1: Working Brand Setup

- Use RangeRivet Works as the working umbrella brand
- Keep ForgeIQ Shopify store/backend unchanged for now
- Use the RangeRivet Works Job Tracker MVP sheet
- Publish a simple GitHub Pages landing page
- Use email or Google Form for intake at first
- Do not build a full app yet

### Phase 2: First 5 Customers

Try to sell:

1. Two garage/shop layout reviews
2. One project plan + material list
3. One job tracker setup
4. One equipment quote review or quote cleanup

Goal:

Learn which offer people understand fastest and which service is easiest to deliver.

### Phase 3: Productize the Winners

Turn repeated work into templates:

- Garage layout intake form
- Project plan checklist
- Job tracker setup sheet
- Quote cleanup checklist
- AI prompt pack
- Equipment quote review checklist

### Phase 4: App Later

Do not build a full app first.

Build the app only after the Google Sheet system proves the workflow.

Future app idea:

- Customer/job folders
- Photo uploads
- Quote drafts
- Invoice drafts
- Status tracking
- Export packets
- AI-assisted job summaries
