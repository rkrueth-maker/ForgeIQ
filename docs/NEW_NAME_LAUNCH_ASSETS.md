A lot of projects do not fail because the idea is bad.

They fail because the details are scattered.

Photos in one place. Measurements somewhere else. Prices not tracked. No clear next step. No simple way to quote it. No system to follow up.

That is what **RangeRivet Works** is being built to help with.

Practical plans. Simple trackers. Clear next actions. Less confusion.

## Best first paid offer

### Project Cleanup Snapshot

Draft price: **$49-$99**

Customer sends:

- Photos
- Notes
- Measurements if available
- Links/products if available
- What they are trying to fix

Deliverable:

- One-page project summary
- Missing information list
- Recommended next step
- Simple action checklist

Why this is the best first offer:

- Easy to deliver
- Low pressure
- Does not require big promises
- Can lead into layout, tracker, workflow, or website work

## Lead workflow

1. New lead enters form.
2. Lead appears in Intake Responses.
3. Owner alert email arrives.
4. Customer confirmation email sends.
5. Open tracker.
6. Read Service Needed, Outcome Wanted, Budget, Details, Priority, and Next Action.
7. Reply with service-specific template.
8. Update status to Contacted.
9. Add notes and follow-up date.

## Do not promise

- Permit-ready structural drawings
- Licensed electrical/plumbing/HVAC work
- Legal advice
- Guaranteed business results
- Exact construction compliance
- Financial guarantees
- Fully automated business with no oversight

Use safe wording:

- planning help
- layout concept
- practical recommendation
- starter tracker
- workflow draft
- estimate organization
- intake setup
- next-step checklist
