FAQ

Before you send the mess.

What is the main business name?

Highway 38 Solutions is the public brand. Highway 38 Solutions LLC can be used later if the business is formally organized that way.

Are the desks separate businesses?

No. Project Desk, Shop Desk, Business Desk, Digital Desk, and Cleanup Desk are service categories under Highway 38 Solutions.

Can I send old texts, screenshots, or half-finished files?

Yes. That is the point. Start with the rough details you already have.

What is not included?

Not legal, tax, permit, licensed engineering, or trade advice. This is planning, organizing, workflow, setup, copy, and practical output work.
