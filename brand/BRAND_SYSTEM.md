# Highway 38 Brand System

Status: original execution-branch assets.

## Parent brand
Highway 38 uses an original blueprint/path mark with two offset diagonal paths. It is intentionally not shaped like an official highway shield and does not imply government affiliation.

## Divisions
- Highway 38 Solutions — copper accent.
- Highway 38 Tools — blue accent.
- Highway 38 Business OS — green accent.
- Highway 38 Supply Co. — brown accent.

## Core colors
- Ink: `#102c3b`
- Copper: `#c7602f`
- Tool blue: `#2e718b`
- OS green: `#365f45`
- Sand: `#f4efe7`
- White: `#ffffff`

## Typography
Use a system sans-serif stack for reliability: Arial, Helvetica, or the platform sans-serif. Do not package or distribute font files.

## Clear space
Keep clear space equal to at least one quarter of the mark width on all sides. Minimum digital mark size is 32 px.

## Usage rules
- Do not place the mark inside a road-shield silhouette.
- Do not add government, DOT, county, or official-route wording.
- Do not distort, rotate, add effects, or change division accent assignment.
- Use the parent mark for favicons and avatars; use the division lockup for division pages and documents.
- Monochrome output may use solid black or white when production limitations require it.
